Pokémon Pokopia is a cozy life-simulation adventure where you play as a human-form Ditto tasked with restoring a desolate wasteland. Instead of battling, you focus on gathering resources and crafting to rebuild the world. It’s a peaceful journey centered on restoration, creativity, and forming deep bonds with the Pokémon that move into your custom-built sanctuaries.

This site is your essential Pokopia companion, organized into three helpful sections. The Main Page introduces core gameplay and basic mechanics to get you started. The Habitats Page provides a detailed index of biome requirements and spawn tips for attracting new Pokémon. Lastly, the Community Page showcases creative player designs and aesthetic layouts to inspire your own building projects.
