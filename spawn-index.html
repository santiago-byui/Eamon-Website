Habitats

Pokemon in Pokopia are drawn to the specific quality of your builds rather than appearing at random. Each habitat triggers a "spawn chack" influenced by the real-time clock. While common types arrive quickly, legendary spawns may take days. Maxamise your efficiency wiht the "Habitat Shuffle" -moving current residents into permanent housing to keep your spawn plots open. This index tracks the exact requierments for the four core biomes to ensure your resources are never wasted.
